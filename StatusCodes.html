Status Codes in Detail

For the payment method Credit Card we provide special card numbers for Amex, DINERS, DISCOVER, JCB, MAESTRO, Mastercard and VISA to test a variety of status codes.

Those status codes you can test (mostly codes that begin with 500.1xxx), are marked bold and provide a link that points directly to the list of all the possible card numbers, 3D and Non-3D, associated with the testable status codes.
